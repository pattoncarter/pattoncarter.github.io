Who Am I?

Get to know me a little better through some of the words myself and others would use to describe me.

  • CREATOR

    I've always been a creator. I've loved to take things apart, see how they work, and put them back together in a new an inventive way. Whether it's coding, hardware, graphic design, or woodworking, I love to create new things just for the experience.

  • LEADER

    Leading is in my blood. Since I was a kid in the Boy Scouts, I've always found myself rising to the top of the pack and leading my peers with compassion and motivation to complete the task at hand.

  • Innovator

    I believe that innovation is integral to advancement, and the ability to take risks in order to create new and ground breaking technologies is a principle I hold dear.

  • Developer

    Since the first computer science course I took during my high school years, I've always been developing. Whether it's something as trivial as a grade compiler or as far reaching as a user interface, I've always turned to coding as a way to solve everyday problems that I'm faced with.

  • Collaborator

    Teamwork introduces new and different perspectives to a problem, and I believe that diverse perspectives can take a project to a higher level. I enjoy being surrounded by peers who are motivated and willing to bring their unique experience to the problem in order to achieve a common goal

  • FOUNDER

    Creation and innovation motivate me, and a way that I've been able to express this motivation is through being the person bold enough to take the first step. Since founding TAMU FUSE, I've realized my passion for taking bold action to fill gaps and solve problems that I observe around me.

  • EXPLORER

    Whether it's backpacking, hiking, kayaking, mountain biking, or road tripping, the leisure activities I gravitate towards always involve exploration of sights and destinations I haven't been exposed to. I love to push outside of my comfort zone in order to peer over the next mountain just to see what's on the other side.

  • ENGINEER

    At my core, I am an engineer. I love to analyze problems and devise unique solutions. It is my passion to make the change that I want to see in this world, and I've found that my outlet to do so is through the creation of out-of-the-box solutions that require real effort and time to create.
