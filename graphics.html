Graphic Design

Promotional Graphic Design

Background

During my Sophomore year I served as the Public Relations Exeutive for TAMU LEGION, a Men's Organization on campus. Throughout the year I created merchandise designs, Instagram Posts, Banners, Signs, etc... for my role.

I taught myself some basic principles of graphic design using Canva and lots of trial and error. I found graphic design to be an outlet for many ideas that rolled around in my head that served a purpose to LEGION. I enjoy designing and use Canva quite frequently in my free time as well as for creating promotional material for TAMU FUSE.
